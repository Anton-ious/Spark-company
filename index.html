How it works

To start marketing your product and newsletter, you must prepare everything related to the product (description, images, files, videos) to explain the product and how it works. Then, you need to upload the information to the site to communicate it to us. After that, we will contact you if the information is clear and actionable.
Steps:

  1. Prepare your product information.
  2. Upload the information to the site.
  3. Wait for our response.
  4. We will contact you if everything is clear.

Note: If you have any questions, you can contact us through the contact page or through our Discord community.
